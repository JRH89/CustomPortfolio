About Me


I'm Jared, a Game & Software Developer based in California. I specialize in programming gameplay mechanics and developing games with Unreal Engine 5 but I also have experience creating apps and websites using vanilla javascript as well as some of its frameworks such as Next.js and Angular.js.

I am a self-taught developer with a passion for creating unique games using Unreal Engine and easy to use software with Python. Although I've only been working in the field for a few months, I've quickly grown a love for developing software that's ad free, engaging, and user-friendly. My goal is to deliver high quality solutions that meet my client's requirements and exceed their expectations.
